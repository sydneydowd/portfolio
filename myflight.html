MyFlight

Currently flyers are forced to source important information related to their trip from multiple places, e.g., passbook for boarding passes, old emails for flight itinerary and flying time, and Google for gate changes or flight delays. MyFlight is a mobile app for frequent flyers that is designed to help take some of the stress out of traveling by providing the user with an easy way to access all their trip information in one simple place.
