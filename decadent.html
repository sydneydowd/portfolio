Decadent Cookies

This package design for Decadent Cookies was geared toward the target audience of children. My goal was to have kids pick these cookies out of all their options when shopping at the grocery store with their parents. When you open the box you open the monsters mouth with the cookies inside. It's a fun way for children to interact with the box and have some fun with friends. I decided to do three versions to gear towards both male and female and have them as collectable items
